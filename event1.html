“Not So Subtle Asian Traits”

Students got to see some of their classmates show off their skills at the Not-So-Subtle Asian Traits talent show. The show was hosted by CantoMando, a group known for their funny and relatable videos on being Asian-American. The two hour show, including 10 acts and a special dance performance, concluded with a meet-and-greet with CantoMando.
